Healthy Pilipinas Awards for Partners 2023 - Extended Until September 27

14 September 2023 | By Healthy Pilipinas

Sali na sa Healthy Pilipinas Awards for Partners 2023!

Pwedeng sumali ang mga Development Partners, Civic Society Organizations, National Government Agencies, at private corporations.

Ang deadline para mag-apply sa Healthy Pilipinas Awards for Partners, extended po muli hanggang Setyembre 27! Ang mga finalists ay iimbitahin sa Awards Night event sa Oktubre 27 sa Manila.

I-download ang application forms mula sa: bit.ly/HPAwards2023

I-submit ang mga applications sa: healthypilipinasawards@doh.gov.ph

PinasLakas na Proteksyon sa Bakunahang Bayan Ngayong Setyembre 26-30, 2023!

22 September 2022 | By Sa Boosters: PinasLakas

Sa Bakunahang Bayan, bawat Pilipino, mas PinasLakas ang proteksyon!

Upang mas paigtingin ang pagbabakuna sa mga senior citizens at booster doses, isasagawa ang Bakunahang Bayan: Special Vaccination Days mula September 26 hanggang 30.

Sa paglungsad ng Bakunahang Bayan, sama-samang kikilos ang buong bayan upang lalo pa nating mapalapit ang bakuna sa bawat Pilipino! Sa ating pagtutulungan, mabubuo natin ang wall of immunity upang patuloy nating pamumuhay nang malaya mula sa paghihigpit sa trabaho, eskwelahan, at mga pasyalan!

5 Creative Ways to Teach Handwashing to Kids

30 September 2022 | By UNICEF

You’ve probably sung the ‘Happy Birthday’ song with your child multiple times by now. But there are other fun ways to teach proper handwashing!

For kids, having a play-based approach in learning handwashing is important, and there are several ways to make it fun and interactive. Messages should be simple such as following the steps “Wet, Lather with soap, Scrub, Rinse and Dry”. It’s also important to remind kids to do handwashing during key times such as before eating, after toilet use and after playing. We list down some activities you can do that can also serve as bonding moments between you and your child.
