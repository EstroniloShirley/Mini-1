About Us

MALIWALO BARANGAY HEALTH CENTER

MALIWALO BARANGAY HEALTH CENTER is the best place to go for medical care. We are a government owned health center located in Tarlac City, Tarlac that provides quality and affordable healthcare services. Our staff of doctors and nurses are committed to providing excellent service with compassion, dignity, respect and confidentiality.
