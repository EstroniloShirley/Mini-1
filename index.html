About Us

What We Do?

We are a community-based health care facility in the Philippines that provides primary health care services to all constituents of the barangay, including first aid, maternal and child health care, communicable diseases, and other basic health services 1. The Barangay Health Center is part of the primary health care system that has been adopted by the Philippines since the 1980s as the main functional method for providing any form of health care for a growing population 1. The Municipal Health Office is responsible for developing the delivery of health services and providing additional budgets for medicines, equipment, and health workers 1. The workers in Barangay Makiling voluntarily render primary health services. They are called Barangay health workers, barangay health officers, Barangay nutrition scholar, and others 1
