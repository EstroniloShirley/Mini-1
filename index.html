Barangay Weekly Health Services

Scheduled patients will be prioritized.

Senior Citizens/PWD Check-up

Schedule: Every Monday| 8:00am - 12nn

Free consultation for Elderly and PWD every monday. To give priority to our elderly people and PWD.

See More

Prenatal Check-up

Schedule: Every Tuesday| 8:00am - 12nn

Prenatal check-up is for all pregnant women to ensure the health of the mother and baby.

Proper monitoring while preganat can prevent miscarriage and other pregnancy complications during prenatal term.

See More

Children Check-up

Schedule: Every Wednesday| 8:00am - 12nn

Check-up for children includes their needed vaccination to ensure their vaccine is complete and upto date.

See More

Adult Health Monitoring Check-up

Schedule: Every Thursday| 8:00am - 12nn

Adult check-up to detect early stage of an illness. Early detection can prevent you to get a serious illness.

See More

Family Check-up / Family Planning

Schedule: Every Friday| 8:00am - 12nn

Family check-up with family planning to monitor your family's health and to educate parents on how to family planning method works.

See More
